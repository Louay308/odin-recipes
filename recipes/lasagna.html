lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a delicious blend of layers of pasta, hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. It’s a perfect comfort food, rich in flavor and satisfying for any occasion. Whether you’re preparing it for a family dinner or a special event, this recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

The secret to its amazing taste lies in the combination of the freshly made meat sauce and the perfect balance of cheeses. You can make it ahead of time and freeze it for later, or enjoy it fresh out of the oven. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a pan and drain excess fat.
  4. Mix the ricotta cheese with eggs, parsley, and half the Parmesan cheese.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and beef. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
  6. Top with mozzarella and bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly.
