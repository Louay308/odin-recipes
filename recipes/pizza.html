Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza recipe features a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and plenty of melty cheese. Customize with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni or other toppings
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce and top with cheese and desired toppings.
  4. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
