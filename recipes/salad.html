Salad Recipe

Description

This fresh salad is a perfect side dish for any meal, combining crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, cucumbers, and a light vinaigrette dressing. Quick, healthy, and full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumbers.
  2. In a bowl, combine the vegetables with olive oil and vinegar.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste and toss well.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.
